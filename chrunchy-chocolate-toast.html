Chrunchy Chocolate ToasT

0
April 4, 2018
Written by mychoiceoffood

Dis can be one of the best chocolate preparation for the chocolate lovers, even I m heartcore chocolate lover n dis so hot , very crunchy ,damn chocolaty toast made my evening!!
The best part is minimum time n ingredient required to make dis mouth tempting chocolate toast!!
Some bread slices, so much of chocolate syrup, amul butter(my ever choice), very little condensed milk, cadbury crumbled , n grilled it for few minutes n here is our dusy brown toast is ready,dats all!!
Ready to eat n enjoy!!
